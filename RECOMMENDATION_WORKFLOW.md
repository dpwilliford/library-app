# Recommendation Workflow

## Submission

Users may submit:
- title
- reason
- course/program relevance
- urgency

## AI Support

AI may:
- add context
- gather evidence
- check collection overlap
- check market availability

## Review

Librarian:
- verifies evidence
- evaluates relevance

## Decision

Head Librarian:
- approve
- reject
- defer

## Logging

All actions must record:
- who
- when
- why
- policy basis
