# Collection Policy

## Purpose

Support teaching and research in media arts:
- comics
- manga/anime
- illustration
- animation
- video games
- film

## Selection Criteria

A work may be selected based on:
- historical importance
- aesthetic/formal relevance
- teaching value
- representation (genre, region, movement)
- creator significance
- student/faculty demand

## Non-Goal

The collection is not exhaustive.

## Title-Level Strategy

For large franchises:
- collect core works
- selectively collect significant adaptations
- avoid redundant editions unless materially distinct

## Material Distinction

A new item should add:
- new content
- new format
- new context
- new pedagogical value

## Balance

The system should track:
- genre distribution
- medium distribution
- geographic distribution
- historical coverage

Imbalances should trigger review.
