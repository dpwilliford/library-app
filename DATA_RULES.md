# Data Rules

## Authority

- Catalog data is authoritative for what the library owns.
- AI-generated data is provisional until reviewed.
- Approved data must be explicitly marked.
- Head librarian decisions are final and logged.

## Evidence Requirement

Every claim must include:
- source
- excerpt or supporting data
- date accessed
- confidence level
- review status

If missing, mark:
- unverified
- provisional

## Required Distinctions

The system must distinguish:
- work
- title cluster
- series
- edition
- translation
- adaptation
- reissue
- special edition
- market listing
- library holding

## Title Genealogy

The system must track:
- origin
- translation history
- adaptation history
- reissue history
- influence
- reception
- teaching relevance

## Selection Rule

A recommendation must justify:
- significance OR
- teaching relevance OR
- collection gap

AND address redundancy.
