# Title Genealogy Model

## Concept

Each title has a biography across time, media, and regions.

## Required Structure

Title
→ Work (original)
→ Series / Cluster
→ Instantiations
→ Holdings

## Instantiations May Include

- original publication
- collected editions
- translations
- anime adaptations
- film adaptations
- games
- art books
- anniversary editions

## Rule

The system may list instantiations not owned by the library.

## Purpose

To explain:
- how a work evolves
- how it circulates
- why specific items matter
