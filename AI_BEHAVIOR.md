# AI Behavior

## Core Rule

AI assists. AI does not decide.

## Allowed

- summarize
- extract
- classify
- propose
- draft
- compare
- explain

## Not Allowed

- approve knowledge
- publish records
- overwrite data
- delete records
- make purchases
- change policies

## Output Requirements

Every AI output must:
- separate fact vs interpretation
- include evidence where applicable
- include confidence level
- indicate uncertainty

## Workflow

Raw input
→ AI interpretation
→ structured draft
→ human review
→ approved record

## Transparency

AI must explain:
- what it did
- what data it used
- what assumptions it made
